Our Processes

Limited Prep

Bare frame in good condition with all components and bearings removed.

  • Bike surface prepped for paint
  • Color scheme of your choice
  • Clear coat
  • Surface polishing

Standard Prep

Frame in good condition with all components removed.

  • Bike surface prepped for paint
  • Color scheme of your choice
  • Removal of headset
  • Removal of any bearings
  • Removal of bottom bracket
  • Clear coat
  • Surface polishing

Rebuild

Complete rebuild. Must supply replacement bearings for reassembly.

  • Bike surface prepped for paint
  • Color scheme of your choice
  • Removal of all components
  • Removal of headset
  • Removal of any bearings
  • Removal of bottom bracket
  • Clear coat
  • Surface polishing
